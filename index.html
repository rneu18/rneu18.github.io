About Me

Rishikesh Neupane

Android Developer

I am an Android Developer with over 5 years of experience in programming, currently leading the Android team for the virtual assistant feature in various Android apps at Fidelity Investments. I have previously worked for companies like JPMorgan Chase, Dick's Sporting Goods, Samsung Research America, and Capital One.

Throughout my career, I have built various mobile and web applications using a diverse range of technologies, including Java, Kotlin, Compose, Coroutines, HTML, JavaScript, React, and various libraries. I have also implemented both MVVM and MVP design patterns in my Android projects.

I am proficient in working in agile environments and have participated in daily scrums, sprint planning meetings, sprint review meetings, and utilized TDD techniques.

My experience spans across a wide range of tools and technologies, including Java, Kotlin, Compose, Coroutines, Jenkins, Retrofit, RxJava, RxAndroid, SQLite, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase, Heroku, Git, Jira, Confluence, GitLab, and more. I am a passionate learner who enjoys staying updated with the latest tools and technologies to develop new and innovative applications.

In addition to my technical expertise, I hold a Master's degrees in Meteorology (M.Sc.) and Rural Development (M.A.) from Tribhuvan University, Nepal, and have completed an intensive certification course in Full Stack Web Development from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, which equipped me with the MERN Stack.

I am an active individual who enjoys playing sports, hiking, and traveling. I have a strong background in cricket, having played for my colleges in Nepal and various teams across the United States.
